DATA ACCESS

The ontology of projects developed by the laboratory, people involved in it, events and meetings can be accessed via a SPARQL endpoint, gracefully provided by dydra.com, or can be downloaded as RDF/XML file.

  • opendatahacklab SPARQL endpoint,
  • opendatahacklab ontology.

VOCABULARIES

The following vocabularies are used in the ontology:

  • FOAF and ORG to represent persons (e.g. developers participating to projects, speakers in some workshop, ...) and organizations, respectively;
  • DOAP for projects, especially those in which software is produced.
  • Event Ontology and for describing meetings and other events involving the laboratory;
  • DCAT for data-sets generated during laboratory activities;
  • LOCN and WSG84 Geo to represent locations.
